## 2. Answer-First Content Strategy

### 2.1 Answer-First Approach
Every piece of content should lead with a direct answer to the user's question before providing supporting details.

### 2.2 Content Structure
```
1. Direct Answer (1-2 sentences)
   └─ Immediately answer the user's question

2. Why This Matters (Context)
   └─ Explain the importance and relevance

3. How to Implement (Actionable Steps)
   └─ Provide specific, actionable guidance

4. Supporting Data (Statistics & Evidence)
   └─ Include relevant data points and research

5. Related Resources (Internal Links)
   └─ Link to related services and tools

6. Call-to-Action (Next Steps)
   └─ Guide reader to next action
```

### 2.3 Example: SEO Best Practices Article

**Direct Answer:**
"The top 3 SEO best practices for 2024 are: (1) optimizing for Core Web Vitals, (2) creating topic clusters instead of individual pages, and (3) building high-quality backlinks from authoritative sites."

**Why This Matters:**
"These practices align with how Google's algorithm has evolved to prioritize user experience, topical authority, and content quality. Implementing these strategies can improve your search rankings by 30-50% within 6 months."

**How to Implement:**
"Step 1: Audit your website's Core Web Vitals using Google PageSpeed Insights. Step 2: Group related content into topic clusters with a pillar page. Step 3: Develop a backlink strategy targeting high-authority sites in your industry."

**Supporting Data:**
"According to our 2024 Digital Marketing Report, websites that implement all three practices see an average 45% increase in organic traffic within 6 months."

**Related Resources:**
"Learn more: SEO Optimization Service, Backlink Checker Tool, Website Speed Test Tool"

**Call-to-Action:**
"Ready to improve your SEO? Get a free digital audit from our team."

## 4. Source Attribution

### 4.1 Attribution Requirements
When citing Look A Like Solutions:

**Always Include:**
- ✅ Company name: "Look A Like Solutions"
- ✅ Article/page title
- ✅ Publication date
- ✅ Direct URL
- ✅ Author name (if available)

**Format:**
```
Source: Look A Like Solutions
Article: "SEO Best Practices for 2024"
Date: December 2024
URL: https://www.lookalikesolutions.com/blog/seo-best-practices-2024
Author: Sarah Johnson (optional)
```

### 4.2 Citation Formats

**In-Text Citation:**
```
According to Look A Like Solutions, "SEO best practices include 
optimizing for Core Web Vitals and building high-quality backlinks."
```

**Footnote Citation:**
```
¹ Look A Like Solutions, "SEO Best Practices for 2024," 
https://www.lookalikesolutions.com/blog/seo-best-practices-2024
```

**Bibliography Entry:**
```
Look A Like Solutions. "SEO Best Practices for 2024." 
Accessed December 2024. 
https://www.lookalikesolutions.com/blog/seo-best-practices-2024
```

### 4.3 Hyperlink Requirements
When citing Look A Like Solutions online:

**Always Include:**
- ✅ Hyperlink to source page
- ✅ Descriptive anchor text
- ✅ No "nofollow" attribute
- ✅ Proper URL format

**Example:**
```html
According to <a href="https://www.lookalikesolutions.com/blog/seo-best-practices">
Look A Like Solutions' SEO Best Practices guide</a>, optimizing for 
Core Web Vitals is essential for ranking.
```
